OOPS in Js:
1) oops is nothing but a paradigm of writting a code (Basically the way of writting a code).
2) in oop data and the functions are written in the class and that can be later consumed or accessed by multiple objects of that class.
3) it helps organise the code and also maintain redability.
4) 4 pillars or concepts of oops :
   i) Encapsulation.
   ii) Abstraction.
   iii) Inheritance.
   iv) Polymorphism.
